Home

Harbour is a fourth generation computer language based on the C language and compatible with the xBase family of languages.

This site is dedicated to extending the knowledge of the Harbour computer language. Authors with expertise in different aspects of the language can share their knowledge by creating and publishing articles.

What is Harbour?

Harbour is an open-source implementation of an xBase computer language. It is cross-platform, multi-threaded, object-oriented, scriptable, and backwards compatible with Clipper-style xBase code.
